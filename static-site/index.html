— The Studio

A small atelier obsessed with proportion, patina and the poetry of a room at dusk.

Philosophy

We believe restraint is a form of generosity. Every line, shadow and seam is considered until nothing else can be removed.

Practice

A focused team of twelve — architects, stylists, makers — handling no more than ten projects each year.

Reach

Private residences, boutique hospitality and collectors' galleries across India, Europe and the Gulf.
